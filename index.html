Overview About My Favourite Movies

"Mrs. Chatterjee vs. Norway" is a compelling film that beautifully portrays the unwavering strength and determination of a mother fighting for her children's rights across two different countries with distinct cultures. Her courage is truly inspiring as she fearlessly takes on the legal systems, even though her emotions occasionally cloud her judgment and prevent her from fully grasping the realities and rules of the world around her. Nevertheless, she remains resolute in her mission to bring her children back home, doing whatever it takes to achieve that goal. It is heartening to watch movies that depict such powerful stories, reminding us of the strength of the human spirit and the lengths a mother will go to protect her family.

"24" is an enthralling film that seamlessly blends elements of thriller and romance. Surya, one of my all-time favorite actors, delivers an outstanding performance in this movie. His exceptional acting skills bring depth and authenticity to his character, captivating the audience throughout the film.

"RRR" is an electrifying film that bursts with boundless energy and showcases breathtaking graphics. Rajamouli's visionary filmmaking leaves viewers speechless, as words simply cannot do justice to the magnificence of this movie.
